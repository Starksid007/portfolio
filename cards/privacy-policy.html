🔐 Privacy Policy

Card Vault — Encrypted Card Details & RuPay Coupon Manager

🛡️ TL;DR: Your data never leaves your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information to any server. Everything runs 100% in your browser.

1. What This App Does

Card Vault is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages that helps you:

  • Store credit/debit card details in AES-256 encrypted form
  • Fetch and manage RuPay coupon codes from your Gmail

There is no backend server. All processing happens locally in your browser.

2. Data Storage Local Only

All your data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage:

  • Card details — encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a PIN-derived key (PBKDF2). The PIN is never stored.
  • Coupon data — cached locally after fetching from Gmail so you can view them offline.
  • Used/unused status — stored locally to track which coupons you've redeemed.
  • Gmail OAuth token — stored temporarily (expires in 1 hour) for API access.

No data is ever sent to any third-party server, analytics service, or database.

3. Gmail Access Read-Only

When you connect your Gmail account, the app requests the gmail.readonly scope, which means:

  • ✅ Can read email messages (to find RuPay coupon emails)
  • ❌ Cannot send, delete, or modify any emails
  • ❌ Cannot access contacts, calendar, or any other Google service

What we search for: Only emails from rupay@truztee.com and rupay@golftripz.com with "Booking ID" in the subject. No other emails are accessed.

Email content is never stored on any server. It is fetched, parsed for coupon codes in your browser, and the raw email data is discarded immediately. Only the extracted coupon details (category, date, code) are cached locally.

4. Google OAuth

Authentication is handled via Google Identity Services (GIS) — Google's official OAuth library. The app:

  • Does not see or store your Google password
  • Receives a temporary access token (valid ~1 hour)
  • Stores the token in localStorage (cleared on logout or expiry)
  • You can revoke access anytime from Google Account Permissions

5. No Data Collection

This app does not use:

  • ❌ Google Analytics or any tracking service
  • ❌ Cookies (except Google's own OAuth cookies)
  • ❌ Any server-side processing or database
  • ❌ Advertising or marketing pixels
  • ❌ Error reporting or crash analytics

6. Security

  • Encryption: Card data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM, a military-grade encryption standard. The encryption key is derived from your PIN using PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations and a random salt.
  • HTTPS: GitHub Pages serves the site over HTTPS, encrypting data in transit.
  • No remote storage: Since data never leaves your device, it cannot be intercepted by a server breach.
  • Open source: The complete source code is publicly available for inspection.

7. Third-Party Services

The only external services used are:

  • Google Gmail API — to search and read coupon emails (with your explicit consent)
  • Google Identity Services — for OAuth authentication
  • GitHub Pages — static file hosting

No other third-party services, SDKs, or libraries are used.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  • Clear your browser's localStorage for this site, or
  • Use the browser's "Clear site data" option in Settings
  • Revoke Gmail access from Google Account Permissions

Since no data exists on any server, clearing your browser data completely removes everything.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for personal use by adults. It is not designed for or directed at children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
